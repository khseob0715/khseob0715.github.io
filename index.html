About me

Hi! My name is Hanseob Kim, and I have an English name, Aiden(unofficially)
I am a beginner researcher interested in VR/AR interaction and image processing deep learning.

When I was an undergraduate, I majored in computer engineering at Chosun University in Korea and was a member of the Visual Computing Lab.
At that time, I developed various VR contents such as travel, medical, simulation, and game.
I thought about the way of the researcher or developer, and decided to study more.
In the meantime, I have a great opportunity, and after I joined KIST's Mixed Reality Lab, I have been working on a master's program at the same time.
I belong to the Digital Experience Institute of Korea University, and I study mobile AR interaction using deep learning as the subject of a master's course.
I have been working on various projects and enjoy developing them.
If you want to know more about me, please refer to the contents below.
